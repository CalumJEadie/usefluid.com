About

Andrew and Calum make up Brinian. We're building collaboration tools for visual thinkers.

We use sketches to communicate every day. Andrew understands both sides of the coin; he read English at St Andrews and has worked as a graphic designer. Calum studied Computer Science at Cambridge, where he created a Visual Programming Language for the Raspberry Pi.

We share a deep empathy for how all kinds of people interact with technology and enthusiasm for attacking user experience problems with design thinking.

Get in touch at hi@usefluid.com and either of @CalumJEadie or @AHTomski.
